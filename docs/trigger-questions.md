# Talk idea trigger questions

## Strong opinions
- What annoys you in your daily work?
- Which "best practice" do you consider overrated?
- What does your team practice that everyone else should do, too?

## Things you have learned the hard way
- What do you know now that you wish you knew 2 years ago
- What went really wrong, and what did you learn from it?
- What technology or method took you a long time to understand and master, and you now can (quite) easily explain

## Things people always ask you about
- Which topics are you the go-to person for a work?
- What do you wish people knew, so they stopped asked you about it?
- What do you wish people asked you about?

## Geeky topics you explore on your spare time
- What rabbit holes on Google do you tend to fall in to on your spare time?
- What do talk about at work that makes colleagues laugh or roll their eyes?
- What have you built or explored just for fun?

## Can you bring an insight from another field?
- Do you have a hobby or a background outside tech that gives you a unique perspective?
- Have you brought an idea from another field into your daily work?
